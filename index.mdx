## Welcome to Karibu APIs

The Karibu APIs enable seamless integration of messaging, verification, and authentication services through SMS, voice (including Swahili voice), and WhatsApp. Engage customers, secure accounts, and build trust with a reliable, multi-channel communication platform tailored for global reach.
